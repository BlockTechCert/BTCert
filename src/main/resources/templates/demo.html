How does the BTCert works

Certificates Publish stage (Intranet)

  1. Student apply new certificate with fill in the basic information
  2. Checker compare and approval the applicant with shcool's record
  3. Issuer merge the certificates to the blockchain transaction and sign it
  4. Admin check the transaction and broadcast it to the peer to peer network

Certificates Verify stage (Internet)

  1. Student provide the certificate to the third party(company,school).
  2. The third party can check the certificate's integrity and authenticity with transparent protocolStart Verify

ABOUT

This system authenticates academic credentials by means of a digital receipt that allows immediate verification by third parties. BTCerts uses blockchain and cryptographic technologies to create a certification infrastructure based on the blockcerts standard.
